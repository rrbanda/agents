# Slide 7: The Rise of Assistants

## Slide Content

### The Next Step Forward

**2020-2022: Assistants Enter the Scene**

They draft emails, refactor code, summarize documents.

They maintain context across conversations.

They become our copilots.

---

**But Here's What We Learned:**

| Capability        | LLM        | Assistant   |
| ----------------- | ---------- | ----------- |
| Understands text  | ✅          | ✅           |
| Maintains context | ⚙️ Limited | ✅           |
| Initiative        | ❌          | ❌           |
| Tool use          | ❌          | ⚙️ Scripted |
| Goal awareness    | ❌          | ❌           |

**The Truth**: Always *reactive*. Convenient, yes. Autonomous, no.
