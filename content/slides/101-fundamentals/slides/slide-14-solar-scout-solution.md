**Visual Elements**:
- Timeline from 7:00 AM to 7:30 AM
- Six workflow steps in sequence
- Arrows showing progression
- Before/After comparison at bottom
- Time stamps clearly visible

**Design Specifications**:
- **Size**: Timeline ~70% of slide, comparison ~20%
- **Colors**: 
  - Each step: Different color (blue gradient)
  - Arrows: Green (progression)
  - Time stamps: White/bold
  - Comparison box: Yellow highlight
- **Shape**: Horizontal timeline with boxes
- **Arrows**: Clear directional flow
- **Typography**: Time stamps large (24pt+), steps clear (18pt+)
