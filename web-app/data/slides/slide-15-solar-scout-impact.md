# Slide 15: Real-World Example: Solar Scout - The Impact

## Slide Content

### The Transformation

**Before:**

Hours of manual research.

Inconsistent updates.

Delayed information.

---

**After:**

Consistent, daily updates.

Tailored to each team.

Always on time.

Frees humans for strategic work.

---

**What Changed**: From reactive to proactive.

**What Stayed**: The quality, the insight, the value.

**What Freed**: Human time for what matters most.

---

**Key Points**:

- Shows real value: consistency, timeliness, personalization
- Frees humans for higher-value work
- The three "What" statements tie it together

**Delivery**:
- Read "Before" with weight (the problem)
- Read "After" with energy (the solution)
